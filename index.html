The MyDrugs Vision

Founded by factornalmex in 2025, MyDrugs is a fictional e-commerce platform inspired by the Netflix series "How to Sell Drugs Online (Fast)".

Our vision was to create a striking digital experience that showcases modern web design principles while paying homage to the unique aesthetic of the show. This fictional marketplace serves as a creative project with absolutely no real products or services offered.

With a focus on sleek, glass-morphism UI elements, neon accents, and a color palette featuring teals, purples, and pinks, we've designed an immersive user experience that feels both futuristic and nostalgic.

The MyDrugs name, branding, and product listings are entirely fictional and are used purely for creative and educational purposes.

David

Founder & CEO

"I wanted to create something that pushes the boundaries of web design while serving as a portfolio piece. MyDrugs represents my vision for how digital experiences can be both beautiful and functional."
